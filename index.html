Wedding
Elevated • romantic

A room filled with blessing

Two paths now meet beneath a canopy of light,
With joy made deep by all who came tonight.
Not only love, but faith and home begun,

A milestone with meaning

A young voice rises strong and clear today,

Warm • reflective

A life built over years

Not every treasure glitters when first found,
Some grow through years of quiet, sacred ground.
A table set, a home, a name made kind,

Questions people usually ask

What changes the final price?
Length, complexity, number of names, amount of specific detail, and whether the piece needs upgraded presentation all affect the final quote.
Is $65 always the final total?
No. It is the base starting price. More detailed assignments can be quoted above that after the request is reviewed.
What format do I receive the poem in?
Every order includes a clean Word file. A designed PDF can be added for a more polished visual presentation.
What kinds of occasions work best?
Weddings, bar and bat mitzvahs, anniversaries, birthdays, baby namings, tribute pieces, dedications, and family keepsake gifts all work well.

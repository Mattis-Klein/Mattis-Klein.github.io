Frequently asked questions

How long does it take to receive my poem?
Most poems are delivered within 3–5 business days. If you have a tight deadline, mention it in your order and we'll do our best to accommodate.
What information do I need to provide?
The occasion, the name(s) to include (English and/or Hebrew), and any themes, memories, or traits you'd like the poem to reflect. The more detail you share, the more personal the poem.
Can I request revisions?
Yes — we want you to love your poem. We offer revisions to make sure the poem feels just right for your simcha.
What does the Designed PDF look like?
It's a professionally laid-out PDF with elegant typography and decorative graphics — ready to print and frame. It costs an additional $25 on top of the poem price.
What occasions do you write poems for?
We write for all simchas: weddings, bar & bat mitzvahs, baby namings, birthdays, anniversaries, sheva brachos, graduations, and more. If you're celebrating, we've got you covered.

Celebration

Today we gather, hearts full of pride,
To celebrate a journey and steps taken with stride.
A name that shines with strength and grace,
A future bright — a steady pace…

Family

In every smile and every song,
You lift the ones you love along.
With warmth and faith as guiding light,
You turn small moments into bright.

Gratitude

For blessings seen and those unseen,
For all that was and all that’s been,
We pause, we thank, we celebrate —
A heart so full, a joy so great.

New Beginnings

A new name whispered, soft and dear,
A world of wonder drawing near.
May every step be blessed with love,
With light that shines from high above.

Marriage

Two paths converge beneath the sky,
Two hearts that chose to journey — why?
Because in you, the other found
A home, a hope, a holy ground.

Legacy

The years have woven stories fine,
Of patience, love, and the divine.
A life well lived, a table set —
A legacy we won't forget.

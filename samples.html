Wedding

Under the chuppah

Tonight the room is warm with song and light,
Two souls brought forward in a moment bright.
Not only joy, but purpose now begins,
A holy home prepared to gather in.

Bar / Bat Mitzvah

A child becoming more

With steady voice and eyes that start to see,
A child steps forward toward responsibility.
The years behind now gather into grace,
And something stronger settles on this place.

Anniversary

Built over time

The years were never empty as they passed,
They shaped a love intended still to last.
A home, a table, stories gently grown,
A legacy no passing year has thrown.

Birthday

Joy around one person

Today we gather not for cake alone,
But for the way your kindness has been shown.
In laughter, warmth, and all the lives you bless,
You leave this world more bright, and nothing less.

Baby naming

A beginning spoken softly

A name is placed with tenderness and care,
A little soul received by love and prayer.
May every step be guided into light,
And every year unfold with blessing bright.

Family tribute

Legacy and gratitude

Some lives grow loud through grandeur and display,
And some through daily goodness, day by day.
The quiet acts, the faith, the table set,
Become the gifts a family won’t forget.
